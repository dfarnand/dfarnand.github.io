Hello there, thank you for taking a look at my blog! I hope to start populating this space with writing. Though by trade I'm a data scientist, my interests span a much wider set of topics. Therefore I see this space as a place where I can publish my thoughts, opinions, and observations on the world at large here, and practice my ability to express myself in writing as well. I certainly don't expect any readers, but if you want to check back (and promise not to judge my posts too harshly), be my guest!

The title of this blog is (tentatively) _Arbitrarities_. The reason for this is simple: there is no reason, I just like the word arbitrary. And it somewhat describes what I'm doing here, incidentally. Its possible I might come up with a better title as time goes on, in which case its subject to change without notice!

Enough for now, stop by again for something new!
